A larger subtraction produces a negative result.

The formula

Convert everything to minutes first

Multiply each hours value by 60, add its minutes, and apply the plus or minus sign. Divide the final total by 60 for decimal hours; divide with a remainder for H:MM.

FAQ

Hours and minutes questions

What is a time duration?

A duration is an amount of elapsed time, such as 2 hours and 30 minutes. It is not a specific time of day.

Can I add more than two durations?

Yes. Add as many rows as you need, and choose whether each duration should be added or subtracted.

Can the minutes value be greater than 59?

Yes. For example, entering 90 minutes is automatically treated as 1 hour and 30 minutes.

How are decimal hours calculated?

The calculator divides the total number of minutes by 60. For example, 7 hours and 15 minutes equals 7.25 decimal hours.

Does this calculator handle dates or time zones?

No. It works only with elapsed durations. Use the main Time Calculator when you need to add or subtract time from a date or clock time.
