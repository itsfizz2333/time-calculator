About

Time math without the friction

Time Calculator is a focused web tool for adding or subtracting days, hours, and minutes. It is designed for people who want an answer quickly, without creating an account or learning a complicated interface.

What this calculator does

Start from the current local time or choose a date and time, then add or subtract a duration. The result updates immediately and can be copied or used as the starting point for another calculation.

What it does not do

This version does not convert between time zones, calculate business days, or account for public holidays. Keeping the scope narrow makes the everyday calculation faster and easier to understand.

Privacy by design

Date and time calculations happen in your browser. The current version does not send or store the dates and times you enter.
