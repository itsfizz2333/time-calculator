FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I add more than 24 hours?

Yes. Open custom duration and add any combination of days, hours, and minutes.

What happens when the result crosses midnight?

The calculator automatically moves to the correct previous or next calendar date.

Does this calculator support leap years?

Yes. It uses your browser’s calendar system, including leap years and the correct number of days in each month.

Does it convert between time zones?

No. This version performs ordinary date and time calculations using your device’s local time.

Are my dates and times saved?

No. The calculation happens in your browser, and this version does not send or store the dates and times you enter.
